A Future Date

From April 21st to the 23rd, we're hosting a free accessibility and inclusive design conference to make up for all the great sessions we had to miss in the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic. This is an all-volunteer effort, and sessions will be captioned.

Make sure to follow us on Twitter for updates, and subscribe to the YouTube channel for a reminder when the conference is about to start. We also have a welcome video with more information.
