A Future Date

Did you miss CSUN ATC, SXSW, or another a11y & inclusive design conference this year because of covid? A Future Date is a volunteer-led virtual event with 3 days of curated sessions that were canceled at major accessibility conferences. Our goal is to continue to build up the a11y community and provide learning opportunities that were missed due to covid-19. Sessions will be captioned.

Make sure to follow us on Twitter for updates, and subscribe to the YouTube channel for a reminder when the conference is about to start. We also have a welcome video with more information.

Tune into the conference from our youtube channel
Many of the speakers will be answering questions in the live chat during their sessions.
